Martin Luther King

Activist and leader

Martin Luther king was african american who advocated social change through nonviolent means. He helped shape the African American Civil Rights Movement of the 1950s and 1960s.He led many peacefu protests and helped african americans get so many rights that they did not have.We are forever thankful for what he did.

Some facts about him are:
  • Martin luther was born in January 15,1929
  • First president of the Southern Christian Leadership Conference
  • We celebrate him every year on Jan 21st to show appreciation
  • He won the Nobel Peace Prize in 1964
  • One of his most famous speech was "I Have A Dream"
  • He used peaceful protests and marches to get his point accross
  • Died on April 9, 1968
